Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your SplitBi account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your SplitBi account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@splitbi.app
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your SplitBi account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and login credentials
  • Expense data: All expenses you have created
  • Group membership: Your membership in all groups
  • Payment records: Records of settlements and payments you've made
Important: Expenses you created that involve other group members may be retained in an anonymized form to maintain accurate balance records for other users. Your personal information will not be associated with this data.

Data Retention

After receiving your deletion request:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Your personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and security purposes
